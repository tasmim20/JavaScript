❓ Difference between Express.js and NestJS

Express.js:
Minimal and flexible. You manage routing, structure, and logic yourself. Best for small or simple APIs.

NestJS:
Structured and opinionated. Uses modules, controllers, and services with dependency injection. Best for large and scalable applications.

⭐ One-line interview answer

Express is lightweight and flexible, while NestJS provides a structured architecture for scalable applications.
